de ltar rnam par thar pa bsam gyis mi khyab pa la gnas pa’i byaṅ chub sems dpa’ ni saṅs rgyas
kyi źiṅ thams cad kyi yon tan bkod pa rnams saṅs rgyas kyi źiṅ gcig tu ston to ||sems can
thams cad kyaṅ lag pa g.yas pa’i mthil du bźag ste | sems kyi mgyogs pa’i rdzu ’phrul gyis
’gro źiṅsaṅs rgyas kyi źiṅ kun tu ston kyaṅsaṅs rgyas kyi źiṅ gcig nas kyaṅ ma g.yos so
||phyogs bcu’i saṅs rgyas bcom ldan ’das la mchod pa ji snyed yod pa de thams cad spu’i khuṅ
bu gcig tu ston to | |phyogs bcu na zla ba daṅ | nyi ma daṅ | skar ma’i gzugs ji snyed
yod pa de thams cad kyaṅ spu’i khuṅ bu gcig tu ston to ||phyogs bcu na rluṅ gi dkyil ’khor
ldaṅ ba ji snyed pa de thams cad kha’i naṅ du brdubs kyaṅde’i lus źig pa yaṅ med ciṅsaṅs
rgyas kyi źiṅ de dag gi rtswa daṅ | nags tshal rnams ñal ba yaṅ med do ||
